What we are

A second look at your vehicle's records — before you accept a denial.

SecondLook is a California lemon-law and vehicle-defect records-analysis company.

When a vehicle keeps having problems, the facts that matter are buried across repair orders, service notes, technician wording, warranty paperwork, messages with the dealer, and — if a claim was denied — the denial letter. Viewed one document at a time, those facts can look like unrelated events. Viewed together, they may reveal a recurring defect pattern.

SecondLook organizes that scattered history into a single, clear timeline and surfaces the patterns, repeat visits, and safety-related details that are easy to miss. We help vehicle owners understand what their own records actually show.

What SecondLook is not. We are not a law firm, and we do not provide legal advice or representation. Our assessments are informational. We are also unrelated to the criminal-justice "second look" / resentencing movement — SecondLook works only on vehicles and California lemon law.

Why "SecondLook"

Most consumers focus on explaining what happened to their car. The people reviewing a claim are often evaluating what was documented. A second look at the records — done carefully and in one place — is where overlooked patterns, missing information, and fixable documentation gaps tend to appear.
